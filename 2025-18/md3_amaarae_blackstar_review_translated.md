# Amaarae - Black Star 专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 英文原文
> There’s a fair amount of sex, drugs, and Young Thug flows on Fountain Baby, Amaarae’s 2023 crystalline dance pop triumph, but Black Star is all sex, drugs, and Young Thug flows. Instead of coasting right into the slot as one of the next pop girls, she blows it all up for a frenetic, ridiculously horny, drugged-out document of self-indulgent partying and not giving a single fuck. Wildly repurposing old pop-rap radio hits, using massive levels of pitch manipulation, and weaving big, glossy spins on rave-ready techno, Brazilian funk, and Jersey club into her Afrobeats foundation, Black Star is a loud and abrasive, incohesive, and out of pocket (c’mon she’s out here riding around in Waymos and comparing her balcony sex to Ruff Ryders rapper Drag-On) romp through the Black diaspora.

I’d understand if you get the itch to compare Black Star to the other ambitious, studied, and dot-connecting pop staples of the recent past. But the truth is that it’s way riskier, more in conversation with the sex-crazed club pop of France’s Theodora and the disruptive 2010s Atlanta homage of Playboi Carti’s MUSIC. By calling her album Black Star and inserting herself on the cover as the black star on the flag of her native Ghana, the record comes with the expectations to not just make a fun Amaarae album but to do justice to that ideological framing. Yet the album feels light and pressure-free, going full steam ahead with ideas that are still taboo in a lot of Black immigrant circles that tend to lean religious—all the coke and ketamine of “Starkilla,” the casualness to how she claims, “My bitch and my nigga the same sex” on “ms60.” She might be the boldest pop star we have right now. –Alphonse Pierre

## 中文翻译
### 逐句翻译
1. 在Amaarae 2023年的水晶般清澈的流行舞曲杰作《Fountain Baby》中，有相当多的性、毒品和Young Thug风格的flow，但《Black Star》全是性、毒品和Young Thug风格的flow。
2. 她没有顺理成章地成为下一批流行女孩中的一员，而是将这一切炸开，创造出一份狂热、极度色情、迷幻的记录，记录了自我放纵的派对和毫不在乎的态度。
3. 她疯狂地重新利用旧的流行说唱电台热门歌曲，使用大量的音高操纵，并将适合狂欢的电子音乐、巴西放克和新泽西俱乐部音乐的华丽改编编织到她的非洲节拍基础中，《Black Star》是一场喧闹、刺耳、不连贯且出格（得了吧，她在这里开着Waymo自动驾驶汽车，把她的阳台性爱比作Ruff Ryders说唱歌手Drag-On）的黑人侨民之旅。
4. 如果你渴望将《Black Star》与最近其他雄心勃勃、经过研究且能够连接点的流行音乐主食进行比较，我能理解。
5. 但事实是，它的风险要大得多，更多地与法国Theodora的性爱狂欢俱乐部流行音乐和Playboi Carti的《MUSIC》中对2010年代亚特兰大的颠覆性致敬进行对话。
6. 通过将她的专辑命名为《Black Star》并将自己作为本国加纳国旗上的黑星放在封面上，这张唱片带来了期望，不仅要制作一张有趣的Amaarae专辑，还要公正地对待那个意识形态框架。
7. 然而，这张专辑感觉轻松无压力，全力以赴地展现了在许多倾向于宗教的黑人移民圈子中仍然被视为禁忌的想法——《Starkilla》中的所有可卡因和氯胺酮，《ms60》中她随意宣称“我的婊子和我的兄弟是同性”的方式。
8. 她可能是我们现在拥有的最大胆的流行歌手。——阿尔方斯·皮埃尔

## 单词短语解析
1. **crystalline dance pop triumph**：水晶般清澈的流行舞曲杰作
   - crystalline：adj. 水晶般的，清澈透明的
   - triumph：n. 胜利，成功，杰作
   - 例句：The album was a crystalline pop triumph that won multiple awards.

2. **Young Thug flows**：Young Thug风格的flow
   - Young Thug：美国说唱歌手，以独特的flow和风格闻名
   - flow：n. 说唱中的节奏和韵律流动
   - 例句：His Young Thug-inspired flows are distinctive and catchy.

3. **coasting right into the slot**：顺理成章地进入位置
   - coast：v. 轻松前进，顺风顺水
   - slot：n. 位置，角色
   - 例句：She could have coasted into the role of a pop star, but chose a different path.

4. **frenetic**：adj. 狂热的，极度兴奋的
   - 例句：The concert had a frenetic energy that kept the crowd dancing.

5. **ridiculously horny**：极度色情的
   - ridiculous：adv. 极其，非常
   - horny：adj. 性欲强烈的
   - 例句：The song's ridiculously horny lyrics caused controversy.

6. **drugged-out**：迷幻的，吸毒后的
   - 例句：The album has a drugged-out atmosphere that reflects the artist's experiences.

7. **self-indulgent partying**：自我放纵的派对
   - self-indulgent：adj. 自我放纵的，任性的
   - 例句：The lyrics describe a lifestyle of self-indulgent partying and excess.

8. **not giving a single fuck**：毫不在乎，完全不在意
   - 例句：She lives her life not giving a single fuck about what others think.

9. **repurposing old pop-rap radio hits**：重新利用旧的流行说唱电台热门歌曲
   - repurpose：v. 重新利用，改变用途
   - 例句：The producer is known for repurposing old samples in new ways.

10. **pitch manipulation**：音高操纵
    - pitch：n. 音高
    - manipulation：n. 操纵，控制
    - 例句：The song uses extensive pitch manipulation to create a unique sound.

11. **weaving big, glossy spins**：编织华丽的改编
    - weave：v. 编织，融合
    - glossy：adj. 华丽的，光滑的
    - spin：n. 改编，重新诠释
    - 例句：The album weaves glossy spins on traditional folk songs.

12. **rave-ready techno**：适合狂欢的电子音乐
    - rave：n. 狂欢派对
    - techno：n. 电子音乐
    - 例句：The DJ played rave-ready techno that kept the crowd dancing all night.

13. **Brazilian funk**：巴西放克音乐，一种源于巴西的节奏强烈的音乐风格
    - 例句：The song incorporates elements of Brazilian funk and electronic music.

14. **Jersey club**：新泽西俱乐部音乐，一种源于美国新泽西州的电子舞曲风格
    - 例句：Jersey club beats are characterized by their fast tempo and energetic rhythm.

15. **Afrobeats foundation**：非洲节拍基础
    - Afrobeats：非洲节拍，一种融合非洲节奏和西方流行音乐的音乐风格
    - 例句：Her music is built on an Afrobeats foundation with modern pop influences.

16. **loud and abrasive**：喧闹且刺耳的
    - abrasive：adj. 刺耳的，磨蚀的
    - 例句：The song's loud and abrasive sound divided critics.

17. **incohesive**：adj. 不连贯的，缺乏一致性的
    - 例句：Some critics found the album incohesive, while others praised its eclecticism.

18. **out of pocket**：出格的，不适当的
    - 例句：Her out of pocket comments caused a stir on social media.

19. **Waymos**：Waymo自动驾驶汽车，Google旗下的自动驾驶汽车公司
    - 例句：She was spotted riding in Waymos around the city.

20. **Ruff Ryders**：Ruff Ryders唱片公司，美国著名的嘻哈唱片公司
    - 例句：DMX was one of the most successful artists signed to Ruff Ryders.

21. **romp through the Black diaspora**：黑人侨民之旅
    - romp：n. 嬉戏，喧闹的活动
    - Black diaspora：黑人侨民，指散居在世界各地的非洲裔人群
    - 例句：The album is a musical romp through the Black diaspora.

22. **dot-connecting pop staples**：能够连接点的流行音乐主食
    - dot-connecting：连接点的，指能够整合不同元素的
    - staples：n. 主食，主要部分
    - 例句：The artist is known for creating dot-connecting pop staples that blend genres.

23. **sex-crazed club pop**：性爱狂欢俱乐部流行音乐
    - sex-crazed：adj. 性爱狂欢的
    - 例句：The song is a prime example of sex-crazed club pop.

24. **disruptive 2010s Atlanta homage**：对2010年代亚特兰大的颠覆性致敬
    - disruptive：adj. 颠覆性的，破坏性的
    - homage：n. 致敬，敬意
    - 例句：The album pays a disruptive homage to 90s hip-hop.

25. **ideological framing**：意识形态框架
    - ideological：adj. 意识形态的
    - framing：n. 框架，结构
    - 例句：The album's ideological framing explores themes of identity and belonging.

26. **taboo in Black immigrant circles**：在黑人移民圈子中被视为禁忌
    - taboo：n. 禁忌，忌讳
    - 例句：Discussing sexuality openly is still taboo in some communities.

27. **tend to lean religious**：倾向于宗教
    - lean：v. 倾向，偏向
    - 例句：The community tends to lean religious in its values and practices.

28. **coke and ketamine**：可卡因和氯胺酮
    - coke：n. 可卡因（俚语）
    - ketamine：n. 氯胺酮，一种麻醉剂和迷幻药
    - 例句：The song references coke and ketamine as part of the party lifestyle.

29. **boldest pop star**：最大胆的流行歌手
    - bold：adj. 大胆的，勇敢的
    - 例句：She is widely regarded as the boldest pop star of her generation.
