# Addison Rae 专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 英文原文
> When Addison Rae was ascending on TikTok with viral dances and beauty deals, the millions that followed her may have never anticipated where she would be five years later. But Rae has become an expert at reminding people to expect the unexpected. Her self-titled debut is energetic and inventive, a true pop fan’s pop album. With touches of Ray of Light Madonna and In the Zone Britney Spears, Addison is meant to get you moving. But it’s the moments she lets you in that help the album shine more brightly, like the nods to family discord and anxiety that peek out of “Times Like These” and “Headphones On.” —B.S.

## 中文翻译
### 逐句翻译
1. 当艾迪生·雷凭借病毒式传播的舞蹈和美妆合作在TikTok上崭露头角时，数百万关注她的人可能从未预料到五年后她会在哪里。
2. 但雷已经成为提醒人们期待意外的专家。
3. 她的同名首张专辑充满活力且富有创意，是一张真正的流行乐迷的流行专辑。
4. 带有《Ray of Light》时期的麦当娜和《In the Zone》时期的布兰妮·斯皮尔斯的风格，《Addison》就是要让你动起来。
5. 但正是她让你走进她内心的时刻，让这张专辑更加闪耀，比如《Times Like These》和《Headphones On》中对家庭不和与焦虑的暗示。——B.S.

## 单词短语解析
1. **ascending**：v. 上升，崭露头角，指在某个领域逐渐获得成功和知名度。
   - 例句：She was ascending in the music industry with her unique style.
2. **viral dances**：病毒式传播的舞蹈，指在社交媒体上迅速流行的舞蹈视频。
   - 例句：The dance challenge went viral on TikTok, with millions of users participating.
3. **beauty deals**：美妆合作，指与美妆品牌的商业合作，如代言、推广等。
   - 例句：The influencer secured several major beauty deals after gaining popularity.
4. **anticipated**：v. 预料，期待，指对未来可能发生的事情的预测。
   - 例句：No one anticipated the success of her debut album.
5. **expect the unexpected**：期待意外，指做好准备面对不可预测的事情。
   - 例句：In this industry, you have to expect the unexpected.
6. **self-titled debut**：同名首张专辑，指以艺人自己名字命名的第一张专辑。
   - 例句：Her self-titled debut album was released last year.
7. **energetic**：adj. 充满活力的，精力充沛的，形容音乐充满动感。
   - 例句：The concert was energetic and kept the audience dancing.
8. **inventive**：adj. 富有创意的，创新的，指音乐风格或制作有新意。
   - 例句：The producer is known for his inventive approach to pop music.
9. **touches of**：带有…的风格，指某种音乐或艺术作品中融入了其他风格的元素。
   - 例句：Her music has touches of jazz and blues.
10. **Ray of Light Madonna**：《Ray of Light》时期的麦当娜，指麦当娜1998年专辑《Ray of Light》时期的音乐风格。
11. **In the Zone Britney Spears**：《In the Zone》时期的布兰妮·斯皮尔斯，指布兰妮2003年专辑《In the Zone》时期的音乐风格。
12. **meant to get you moving**：就是要让你动起来，指音乐的目的是激发听众的活力，让他们跳舞。
13. **lets you in**：让你走进她内心，指艺人在音乐中展现真实的情感和个人经历。
14. **shine more brightly**：更加闪耀，指专辑中某些部分特别出色，突出。
15. **nods to**：对…的暗示，指在音乐中提及或引用某个主题或情感。
   - 例句：The song contains nods to his childhood memories.
16. **family discord**：家庭不和，指家庭成员之间的矛盾或冲突。
   - 例句：The lyrics touch on themes of family discord and reconciliation.
17. **anxiety**：n. 焦虑，指内心的不安和担忧。
   - 例句：Many young artists struggle with anxiety in the spotlight.
18. **peek out of**：从…中显现，指某种情感或主题在歌曲中隐约出现。
   - 例句：Hints of sadness peek out of the upbeat melody.
