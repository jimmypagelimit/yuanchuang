# Wet Leg《moisturizer》专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 原文（c:/code/yuanchuang/2025-18/md3.md 第207行）
> Maybe that 80-quid pot of Drunk Elephant really can save you: moisturizer totally transformed Wet Leg, turning the Isle of Wight five-piece into a band as funny, acerbic, and clever as NME always said they were. The primary fuel for this album was frontwoman Rhian Teasdale discovering she was queer and, subsequently, falling in love, and it finds endless joy, tension, and entertainment in those things: “catch these fists” and “mangetout” gleefully turn the epithet “man-hating lesbian” into a badge of honor; “jennifer’s body” and “davina mccall” make having a crush sound as sweaty and intense as it actually is, while “pillow talk” is so horned-up that it would make most self-professed gooners blush. That track gets to the core message of moisturizer: Fake freaks need not apply. –Shaad D’Souza

## 逐句翻译与解析

### 句子1
**原文**：Maybe that 80-quid pot of Drunk Elephant really can save you: moisturizer totally transformed Wet Leg, turning the Isle of Wight five-piece into a band as funny, acerbic, and clever as NME always said they were.

**翻译**：也许那罐80英镑的Drunk Elephant真的能拯救你：《moisturizer》彻底改变了Wet Leg乐队，将这支来自怀特岛的五人乐队转变为一支正如NME一直所说的那样有趣、尖刻且聪明的乐队。

**解析**：
- **80-quid pot**: 80英镑的罐子，quid是英国口语中对英镑的称呼
- **Drunk Elephant**: 美国高端护肤品牌，这里用护肤品比喻专辑名称《moisturizer》
- **moisturizer**: 保湿霜，这里是专辑名称
- **totally transformed**: 彻底改变
- **Isle of Wight**: 怀特岛，英国南部岛屿
- **five-piece**: 五人乐队
- **NME**: New Musical Express，英国著名音乐杂志

### 句子2
**原文**：The primary fuel for this album was frontwoman Rhian Teasdale discovering she was queer and, subsequently, falling in love, and it finds endless joy, tension, and entertainment in those things.

**翻译**：这张专辑的主要创作动力是主唱Rhian Teasdale发现自己是酷儿，以及随后陷入爱河的经历，专辑在这些事情中找到了无尽的快乐、紧张感和娱乐性。

**解析**：
- **primary fuel**: 主要燃料，这里指创作动力
- **frontwoman**: 女主唱
- **queer**: 酷儿，指非异性恋身份
- **subsequently**: 随后
- **finds endless joy, tension, and entertainment**: 找到了无尽的快乐、紧张感和娱乐性

### 句子3
**原文**：“catch these fists” and “mangetout” gleefully turn the epithet “man-hating lesbian” into a badge of honor; “jennifer’s body” and “davina mccall” make having a crush sound as sweaty and intense as it actually is, while “pillow talk” is so horned-up that it would make most self-professed gooners blush.

**翻译**：《catch these fists》和《mangetout》欢快地将"仇视男性的女同性恋"这一绰号转变为荣誉徽章；《jennifer’s body》和《davina mccall》让暗恋听起来像现实中一样汗流浃背且紧张刺激，而《pillow talk》则充满了强烈的欲望，会让大多数自称色迷迷的人都脸红。

**解析**：
- **catch these fists**: 专辑中的歌曲名称《接住这些拳头》
- **mangetout**: 专辑中的歌曲名称《嫩豌豆》（法语，指连豆荚一起吃的豌豆）
- **gleefully**: 欢快地
- **epithet**: 绰号，称号
- **badge of honor**: 荣誉徽章
- **jennifer’s body**: 专辑中的歌曲名称《詹妮弗的身体》（可能参考了同名电影）
- **davina mccall**: 专辑中的歌曲名称《达维娜·麦考尔》（英国著名电视主持人）
- **having a crush**: 暗恋，迷恋
- **sweaty and intense**: 汗流浃背且紧张刺激
- **pillow talk**: 专辑中的歌曲名称《枕边细语》
- **horned-up**: 充满欲望的（俚语）
- **self-professed gooners**: 自称色迷迷的人，gooner是俚语，指好色之徒
- **blush**: 脸红

### 句子4
**原文**：That track gets to the core message of moisturizer: Fake freaks need not apply.

**翻译**：那首歌道出了《moisturizer》的核心信息：伪怪人勿扰。

**解析**：
- **That track**: 指前面提到的《pillow talk》
- **gets to the core message**: 道出了核心信息
- **Fake freaks need not apply**: 伪怪人勿扰，这是一种幽默的说法，强调专辑的真实性和独特性

### 句子5
**原文**：–Shaad D’Souza

**翻译**：——沙德·德索萨（乐评人）

**解析**：
- **Shaad D’Souza**: 乐评人的名字，翻译时保留原名并注明身份
