# Amaarae - Black Star 专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 英文原文
> On her third album, boundary-busting singer Amaarae dives straight into the club. From the stalagmite synths of leadoff track “Stuck Up,” Black Star is a sweaty, lusty thrill ride with Amaarae firmly in control of the pleasure sensors — for her and for any amorous targets she might pick up. Bringing along a VVIP list of guests — among them bedroom auteur PinkPantheress, funk legend Charlie Wilson, and hyperpop visionary Bree Runway — she whirls through dance music’s past and future with bravado and bluster. —M. Johnston

## 中文翻译
### 逐句翻译
1. 在她的第三张专辑中，突破界限的歌手Amaarae直接冲进了俱乐部。
2. 从主打曲《Stuck Up》中如石笋般的合成器，《Black Star》是一场汗水淋漓、充满欲望的刺激之旅，Amaarae牢牢控制着快乐传感器——无论是为她自己还是为任何她可能吸引的多情目标。
3. 她带来了一份VVIP嘉宾名单——其中包括卧室创作人PinkPantheress、放克传奇Charlie Wilson和超流行音乐愿景家Bree Runway——她带着狂妄和自信穿梭于舞曲音乐的过去和未来。——M. 约翰斯顿

## 单词短语解析
1. **boundary-busting singer**：突破界限的歌手
   - boundary-busting：突破界限的，打破常规的
   - boundary：n. 界限，边界
   - busting：v. 打破，突破
   - 例句：She's known as a boundary-busting artist who constantly pushes creative limits.

2. **dives straight into the club**：直接冲进俱乐部
   - dive into：冲进，投身于
   - straight：adv. 直接地，立刻
   - 例句：The album dives straight into upbeat rhythms from the first track.

3. **stalagmite synths**：如石笋般的合成器
   - stalagmite：n. 石笋（从洞穴底部向上生长的碳酸钙沉积）
   - synth：n. 合成器（synthesizer的缩写）
   - 例句：The song features dramatic stalagmite synths that build throughout the track.

4. **leadoff track**：主打曲，开场曲
   - leadoff：adj. 开场的，首先的
   - track：n. 歌曲，曲目
   - 例句："Stuck Up" serves as the leadoff track on her new album.

5. **sweaty, lusty thrill ride**：汗水淋漓、充满欲望的刺激之旅
   - sweaty：adj. 出汗的，汗水淋漓的
   - lusty：adj. 充满欲望的，精力充沛的
   - thrill ride：刺激之旅，惊险体验
   - 例句：The concert was a sweaty, lusty thrill ride that left the audience exhausted but exhilarated.

6. **pleasure sensors**：快乐传感器
   - pleasure：n. 快乐，愉悦
   - sensor：n. 传感器
   - 例句：The music seems designed to activate pleasure sensors in the brain.

7. **amorous targets**：多情目标，恋爱对象
   - amorous：adj. 多情的，恋爱的
   - target：n. 目标，对象
   - 例句：The lyrics describe the singer's amorous targets at the club.

8. **pick up**：吸引，搭讪
   - 例句：He was trying to pick up women at the bar.

9. **VVIP list of guests**：VVIP嘉宾名单
   - VVIP：Very Very Important Person，超级重要人物
   - 例句：The event had a VVIP list of celebrities and industry insiders.

10. **bedroom auteur**：卧室创作人
    - bedroom：adj. 卧室的（指在家中创作的）
    - auteur：n. 创作者，艺术家
    - 例句：She started as a bedroom auteur making music in her home studio.

11. **funk legend**：放克传奇
    - funk：n. 放克音乐（一种源于美国的节奏强烈的音乐风格）
    - legend：n. 传奇，传奇人物
    - 例句：Charlie Wilson is widely regarded as a funk legend.

12. **hyperpop visionary**：超流行音乐愿景家
    - hyperpop：n. 超流行音乐（一种融合流行、电子和实验元素的音乐风格）
    - visionary：n. 有远见的人，愿景家
    - 例句：Bree Runway is considered a hyperpop visionary for her innovative sound.

13. **whirls through**：穿梭于，快速浏览
    - whirl：v. 旋转，快速移动
    - 例句：The album whirls through different musical genres with ease.

14. **bravado and bluster**：狂妄和自信，虚张声势
    - bravado：n. 狂妄，虚张声势
    - bluster：n. 咆哮，夸夸其谈
    - 例句：His performance was full of bravado and bluster that captivated the audience.
