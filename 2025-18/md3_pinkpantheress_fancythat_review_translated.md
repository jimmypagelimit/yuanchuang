# PinkPantheress《Fancy That》专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 原始英文文本
> Like many girls who came of age in the aughts and early 2010s, PinkPantheress spent plenty of time reading fan fiction. A classic Pink track feels like it could have been lifted straight from a self-insert, the kind where the protagonist meticulously shaves her entire body on the off chance that she’s invited backstage to hook up with the band. On her second album, Fancy That, she’s evolved from brooding over unrequited love atop breakbeats to embracing a much more flirtatious and forward tone. “Come talk to me. Do you want sex with me?” she whispers on “Tonight.” You can almost picture her twirling a fluffy pastel gel pen, feet kicking in the air, as she writes in her glittery Girl Tech Password-protected journal.

## 中文翻译与解析

### 逐句翻译
1. Like many girls who came of age in the aughts and early 2010s, PinkPantheress spent plenty of time reading fan fiction.
   > 和许多在2000年代和2010年代初成年的女孩一样，PinkPantheress花了大量时间阅读同人小说。

2. A classic Pink track feels like it could have been lifted straight from a self-insert, the kind where the protagonist meticulously shaves her entire body on the off chance that she’s invited backstage to hook up with the band.
   > 一首经典的PinkPantheress曲目感觉就像直接从一部自我代入的同人小说中摘出来的，那种主角会一丝不苟地剃光全身，万一有机会被邀请到后台与乐队勾搭。

3. On her second album, Fancy That, she’s evolved from brooding over unrequited love atop breakbeats to embracing a much more flirtatious and forward tone.
   > 在她的第二张专辑《Fancy That》中，她已经从在碎拍音乐上忧郁地唱着单恋，演变为拥抱一种更加调情和直接的语调。

4. “Come talk to me. Do you want sex with me?” she whispers on “Tonight.”
   > “来和我谈谈。你想和我做爱吗？”她在《Tonight》这首歌中低语道。

5. You can almost picture her twirling a fluffy pastel gel pen, feet kicking in the air, as she writes in her glittery Girl Tech Password-protected journal.
   > 你几乎可以想象她用一支毛茸茸的柔和色凝胶笔旋转着，双脚在空中踢着，同时在她那本闪闪发光的、有Girl Tech密码保护的日记本上写着。

### 单词短语解析

1. **PinkPantheress** - 英国歌手、词曲作者，以其独特的声音和复古的音乐风格而闻名
2. **came of age** - 成年，指达到法定年龄或在心理上成熟
3. **aughts** - 2000年代（2000-2009年）
4. **fan fiction** - 同人小说，由粉丝创作的基于现有作品的文学作品
5. **self-insert** - 自我代入，指在小说中插入一个代表作者自己的角色
6. **protagonist** - 主角，故事的主要人物
7. **meticulously** - 一丝不苟地，非常仔细地
8. **on the off chance** - 万一，可能性很小的情况下
9. **backstage** - 后台，舞台后面的区域
10. **hook up with** - 与...勾搭，发生性关系
11. **Fancy That** - 专辑名，可译为《想想看》或保留原名
12. **brooding over** - 忧郁地思考，沉思
13. **unrequited love** - 单恋，得不到回报的爱
14. **breakbeats** - 碎拍，一种常见于嘻哈和电子音乐的节奏模式
15. **flirtatious** - 调情的，卖弄风情的
16. **forward** - 直接的，坦率的
17. **whispers** - 低语，轻声说
18. **twirling** - 旋转，转动
19. **fluffy pastel gel pen** - 毛茸茸的柔和色凝胶笔，一种色彩柔和、笔身可能有绒毛装饰的文具
20. **glittery** - 闪闪发光的
21. **Girl Tech** - 一种面向女孩的电子玩具品牌，这里指带有密码保护功能的日记本
