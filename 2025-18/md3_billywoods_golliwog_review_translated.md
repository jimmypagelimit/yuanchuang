# billy woods《GOLLIWOG》专辑乐评翻译与解析

## 原始英文文本
> Each song on billy woods’ GOLLIWOG unfolds with the knowledge that death, or perhaps a fate worse than, waits behind every corner. His characters stare up at planes as bomb hatches swing open, or wake from nightmares, only to find themselves trapped in coffins. They’re threatened by vampires, soulless record executives, corrupt politicians, abusive fathers, and the American military apparatus, and woods cleverly constructs the moments where they know it’s all fucked. He’s approached this subject matter throughout his career, but rarely sustains that bullet-sweating, white knuckle tension for an entire LP. Here, he writes like an amalgam of Shirley Jackson, Mariana Enríquez, and Frank B. Wilderson III, crafting a speculative opus titled mere degrees from our reality. It’s harrowing and horrific, the cathartic laughter after the jump scare catching in our throats. –Dash Lewis

## 中文翻译与解析

### 逐句翻译
1. Each song on billy woods’ GOLLIWOG unfolds with the knowledge that death, or perhaps a fate worse than, waits behind every corner.
   > 在billy woods的专辑《GOLLIWOG》中，每首歌都带着这样一种认知展开：死亡，或者可能更糟的命运，潜伏在每个角落。

2. His characters stare up at planes as bomb hatches swing open, or wake from nightmares, only to find themselves trapped in coffins.
   > 他笔下的角色仰望轰炸机舱门开启的飞机，或从噩梦中惊醒，却发现自己被困在棺材里。

3. They’re threatened by vampires, soulless record executives, corrupt politicians, abusive fathers, and the American military apparatus, and woods cleverly constructs the moments where they know it’s all fucked.
   > 他们受到吸血鬼、冷酷无情的唱片公司高管、腐败政客、虐待成性的父亲和美国军事机构的威胁，而woods巧妙地构建了他们意识到一切都完了的时刻。

4. He’s approached this subject matter throughout his career, but rarely sustains that bullet-sweating, white knuckle tension for an entire LP.
   > 在他的职业生涯中，他一直在探讨这个主题，但很少能在整张专辑中保持那种令人汗流浃背、紧张到指节发白的氛围。

5. Here, he writes like an amalgam of Shirley Jackson, Mariana Enríquez, and Frank B. Wilderson III, crafting a speculative opus titled mere degrees from our reality.
   > 在这里，他的写作风格融合了Shirley Jackson、Mariana Enríquez和Frank B. Wilderson III的特点，创作了一部与我们的现实仅有几度之差的思辨性杰作。

6. It’s harrowing and horrific, the cathartic laughter after the jump scare catching in our throats. –Dash Lewis
   > 这张专辑令人痛苦和恐惧，就像在 jump scare（突然惊吓）之后，本应宣泄情绪的笑声却卡在了喉咙里。–Dash Lewis（乐评人）

### 单词短语解析

1. **billy woods** - 美国 underground hip-hop 艺术家，以富有诗意和政治意味的歌词闻名
2. **GOLLIWOG** - 专辑名，需注意这个词在历史上有种族主义内涵，是对非洲裔美国人的刻板形象描绘
3. **unfolds** - 展开，呈现
4. **fate worse than (death)** - 比死亡更糟的命运
5. **bomb hatches swing open** - 炸弹舱门开启，形容战争或暴力场景
6. **wake from nightmares, only to find** - 从噩梦中醒来，却发现（更糟糕的现实）
7. **vampires** - 吸血鬼，这里比喻剥削者
8. **soulless record executives** - 冷酷无情的唱片公司高管
9. **corrupt politicians** - 腐败政客
10. **abusive fathers** - 虐待成性的父亲
11. **American military apparatus** - 美国军事机构
12. **it's all fucked** - 一切都完了，一切都糟透了（口语表达）
13. **bullet-sweating** - 紧张到出汗，形容极度紧张
14. **white knuckle tension** - 指节发白的紧张，形容极度紧张
15. **LP** - Long Play，密纹唱片，这里指专辑
16. **amalgam** - 混合物，融合体
17. **Shirley Jackson** - 雪莉·杰克逊，美国恐怖小说作家，以《山屋惊魂》闻名
18. **Mariana Enríquez** - 玛丽安娜·恩里克斯，阿根廷作家，以恐怖和超自然小说闻名
19. **Frank B. Wilderson III** - 弗兰克·B·怀尔德森三世，美国作家、学者，研究种族和黑人研究
20. **speculative opus** - 思辨性杰作，speculative指基于推测或想象的
21. **mere degrees from our reality** - 与我们的现实仅有几度之差，指非常接近现实
22. **harrowing** - 令人痛苦的，折磨人的
23. **horrific** - 令人恐惧的，可怕的
24. **cathartic laughter** - 宣泄情绪的笑声，cathartic指宣泄情感的
25. **jump scare** - 突然惊吓，恐怖作品中突然出现的吓人元素
